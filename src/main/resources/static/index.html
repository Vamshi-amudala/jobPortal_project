Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I create an account? +
Click the "Register" button in the top right corner, fill out the form with your details, and verify your email address to get started.
Is JobPortal free to use? +
Yes, creating an account and searching for jobs is completely free. We also offer premium features for enhanced job search capabilities.
How do I apply for jobs? +
Browse job listings, click on positions that interest you, and use the "Apply Now" button. You can apply with your saved profile or upload specific documents.
Can employers contact me directly? +
Yes, employers can reach out to you through our secure messaging system if your profile matches their requirements.
